What is this?

The situation is as follows: We are monitoring a charging park owned by the companies listed. Each company has time intervals during which they'd like access to the park (randomly generated data). We have to select no more than one job from each company such that no two jobs have overlapping time intervals, with our goal being to include as many companies as possible.
