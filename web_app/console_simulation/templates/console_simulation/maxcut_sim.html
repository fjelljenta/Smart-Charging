What is this?

The 5 listed vehicles are at a charging station with 4 charging ports. Each vehicle has its own randomly generated charging time and 'importance'. An ambulance for example has a higher 'importance' than a regular car. The app tries to find the ideal way to assign them different charging ports. The way this distribution is found is by using a quantum algorithm simulated classically. Click here to learn more.
